LINUS GABRIEL SEBASTIAN

A selfie turned into a meme

Linus Sebastian is a Canadian YouTube personality, best known for creating and hosting four technology YouTube channels, Linus Tech Tips (LTT), Techquickie, TechLinked, and ShortCircuit , which have a combined subscriber base of over 20 million. From 2007 to 2015, he was a regular presenter of technology videos for the now defunct Canadian computer retailer NCIX. He is also the owner of Floatplane Media.

As of March 2021, Linus Tech Tips is ranked the most-watched technology channel on YouTube. In 2014, Tubefilter named the channel as being within the "top 1% of Google's preferred advertising channels" on YouTube for the technology category. In 2015, Inc. magazine ranked Sebastian fourth in a list of the "Top 30 Power Players in Tech".

Notable Events

Apart from leading his channel to push the limits of tech such as building a computer capable of supporting 7 individual users, a machine capable of gaming at 16k resolution, and creating an 18 carat gold Xbox Series X controller,Sebastian has gained notoriety for things such as his face and rections becoming memes and dropping expensive and rare pieces of tech on more than one occasion.
